Facebook Data Architecture Outline

Users
  has_many posts
  has_many friends, class_name User
  has_many comments

  name:string [unique, presence, 4-12 chars, format]
  password:string [presence, 6-20 chars, format]
  email:string

Posts
  belongs_to user
  has_many comments
  
  title:string [presence, 1-150 chars]
  body:text

Comments
  belongs_to posts
  belongs_to user through posts

  body:text [presence]
